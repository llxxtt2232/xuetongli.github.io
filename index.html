👋 About Me

Hello! I am currently a Ph.D. candidate in the Intelligence Science and Technology program at the Shanghai Institute of Intelligent Autonomous Systems (affiliated with Tongji University). My research focuses on the interdisciplinary intersection of artificial intelligence and engineering, with particular emphasis on Multimodal Large Language Models (MLLMs), Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG) for engineering applications, Graph Neural Networks (GNNs), Knowledge Graphs (KGs), and Multi‑Agent collaborative systems. My overarching goal is to build an intelligent management and control framework that spans the entire lifecycle of engineering projects.

✦

Research Interests 研究方向

  • Multi-modal Large Language Models (MLLM) for bridge infrastructure（面向桥梁基础设施多模态大语言模型）
  • Engineering Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG) & industry vector knowledge base construction（工程检索增强生成RAG、行业向量知识库构建）
  • GNN automated construction for bridge maintenance knowledge graphs（GNN图神经网络+桥梁运维知识图谱自动化构建）
  • Human-in-the-loop framework & multi-agent collaborative decision systems（人在回路智能框架、多智能体协同决策系统）
  • Unstructured engineering text extraction & structural defect intelligent diagnosis（工程非结构化文本抽取、结构病害智能研判）
